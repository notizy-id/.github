## 🚀 About Notizy

Notizy is an innovative SaaS platform that brings AI-powered customer service agents directly into the WhatsApp ecosystem. Specifically engineered to operate as a Meta Tech Partner platform, Notizy empowers businesses to automate their customer interactions intelligently, personally, and efficiently.

## ✨ Key Features

We are actively building Notizy to provide a comprehensive and seamless customer service solution. Core features include:

*   **Agentic AI:** Utilizing advanced, stateful AI agents to autonomously handle customer inquiries, resolve complaints, and provide human-like assistance around the clock.
*   **Broadcast System:** Enabling businesses to effortlessly send mass messages and targeted marketing campaigns directly to their customers' WhatsApp.
*   **Multi-user Dashboard:** A centralized, collaborative control panel allowing multiple team members to monitor, manage, and step into conversations simultaneously.

## 📈 Project Status

Notizy is currently in **active development**. Our current focus is on refining the AI integration, ensuring seamless automated conversation flows, and building a rock-solid, scalable dashboard for our users.
